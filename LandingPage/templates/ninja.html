Here is some cool information about ninjas!

  • The word ninja means “a person who endures or spies.”
  • “One night, under the cover of rain and wind, Hachiman-yama was approached by a highly skilled shinobi who set fire to the temple.”
  • That was first mention of a ninja in history
  • One the most famous ninja of all time was Hattori Hanzo, who was recruited by the Tokugawa family as a samurai general.
  • Their clothes may have held a set of concealed weapons, but in general ninja looked like normal people.
